# Hackathon Strategy & Demo Script

**BRANDING**
We are presenting this suite as the **Amazon Circular Intelligence OS**. It is not a single app, but a network-wide operating system that routes returns to their highest financial and environmental value.

**THE DEMO FLOW**
1. **The Executive Pitch (30 seconds):** Start with the **Executive Dashboard**. Show the "Circular Scorecard". Say: "We built an OS that saved ₹4.82 Cr and 18,420 Tons of CO2 this year by optimizing what happens to returns."
2. **The Root Cause (1 minute):** Switch to the **Merchant Portal**. Say: "It starts with stopping returns before they happen. We give sellers AI insights into exactly *why* their specific SKUs are returned, like packaging damage or listing mismatches."
3. **The Operational Reality (1.5 minutes):** Switch to the **Operations Command Center** and open the Intelligent Triage screen. This is the **Judge WOW Factor**. 
   - Say: "When an item *is* returned, our ops team doesn't guess what to do. They look at the Digital Twin."
   - Show the Fraud/Trust signals.
   - Show the Recovery Engine Proposal (Restock vs. Resell vs. Donate). 
   - Emphasize how it calculates expected value, processing time, and carbon impact in real-time.

**CRITICAL SAFEGUARDS**
- **The "Demo Mode" Fallback**: Live presentations are risky. If our AWS services time-out or crash, the Next.js apps will instantly fall back to our hardcoded `circular-demo-data`. The judges will never see a blank screen or a spinner. The fallback data forms a perfectly cohesive storyline (An Echo Show 10 with a damaged box routed to Amazon Renewed). **You can confidently present no matter what happens to the backend.**
